### Selected Projects (Pharm Sci and AI/ML Focus)

- **[PGx Warfarin ML Portfolio](https://github.com/Uche-Stephen/pgx-warfarin-ml-portfolio)**  
  End-to-end pharmacogenomic dosing pipeline built on IWPC-style data. Covers ETL, feature engineering for clinical and genetic covariates, multiple ML baselines, and SHAP-based interpretability to understand how variants and patient factors shift dose recommendations.  
  *Stack: Python, scikit-learn, SHAP, Streamlit*

- **[Gentamicin PK and ML Dosing Model](https://github.com/Uche-Stephen/gentamicin-pk-ml)**  
  Hybrid pharmacokinetic and machine learning workflow that uses patient covariates and PK principles to classify therapeutic versus non-therapeutic concentrations and support initial dosing decisions. Shows how ML can complement classical dosing nomograms in antimicrobial therapy using a one-compartment gentamicin model and a RandomForest classifier.  
  *Stack: Python, scikit-learn, Streamlit*

- **[RNA-seq Differential Expression for Drug Response Biology](https://github.com/Uche-Stephen/rnaseq_differential_expression)**  
  RNA-seq workflow from QC through differential expression and volcano plots, designed to explore how gene expression programs may relate to treatment effects and toxicity. Supports GEO downloads and local supplementary tables, with clear outputs and figures.  
  *Stack: Python, GEOparse, matplotlib*

- **[Gene Expression Classifier for Disease States](https://github.com/Uche-Stephen/gene-expression-classifier)**  
  Supervised learning on gene expression matrices, with calibrated probabilities and basic error analysis, showing how omics signals can be incorporated into therapeutic decision support and patient stratification.  
  *Stack: Python, scikit-learn*

- **[Diabetes Clinical ML Portfolio](https://github.com/Uche-Stephen/diabetes-ml-portfolio)**  
  Clinical tabular data project focusing on risk prediction and stratification in diabetes. Emphasizes feature importance, fair evaluation, and how such models might integrate into medication review, follow-up planning, and identification of high-risk patients.  
  *Stack: Python, scikit-learn, pandas*

- **[DNA Sequence Explorer](https://github.com/Uche-Stephen/dna-sequence-explorer)**  
  Command-line tool for GC content, codon usage, and motif scans, with tests and a clean interface. Intended as a reusable component for pharmacogenomic and regulatory variant exploration.  
  *Stack: Python, Biopython*

- **Other Utilities**  
  - **[Data Dashboard](https://github.com/Uche-Stephen/data_dashboard)**  
    Lightweight analytics dashboard built with Streamlit, pandas, and Plotly to explore and visualize tabular clinical or omics data.  
  - **[Automated File Organizer](https://github.com/Uche-Stephen/automated_file_organizer)**  
    Utility script that automatically sorts files into labeled folders, with dry-run and undo support and a clean CLI.
